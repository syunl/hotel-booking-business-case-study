# Hotel Booking Demand Analysis
### Business Case Study for Agoda Strategic Account Manager

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a public hotel booking dataset to demonstrate my analytical approach to solving business problems relevant to online travel platforms.

Although this dataset is different from Agoda's internal data, the objective is to showcase how I use data to identify business opportunities, generate actionable insights, and support data-driven decision making.

---

## Business Objective

The purpose of this project is to answer several business questions such as:

- Which months generate the highest revenue?
- Which customer segments have the highest cancellation rates?
- How does booking lead time affect cancellations?
- Which market segments contribute the most revenue?
- What recommendations can help hotel partners improve performance?

---

## Dataset

- Source: Hotel Booking Demand Dataset (Kaggle)
- Records: ~119,000 hotel bookings
- Hotel Types:
  - Resort Hotel
  - City Hotel

---

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Seasonal Demand Analysis

- Booking volume by month
- Average Daily Rate (ADR)
- Cancellation Rate

Business Goal:
Identify peak seasons and optimize pricing strategies.

---

### 2. Market Segment Analysis

Compared different customer acquisition channels:

- Online TA
- Direct
- Corporate
- Groups
- Offline TA/TO

Metrics:

- Booking Volume
- Cancellation Rate
- ADR
- Lead Time

Business Goal:
Understand partner performance and optimize distribution strategy.

---

### 3. Lead Time Analysis

Investigated how booking lead time influences cancellation probability.

Business Goal:
Reduce cancellation risk through pricing and booking policies.

---

### 4. Revenue Contribution Analysis

Estimated revenue contribution by market segment.

Business Goal:
Identify the most valuable customer channels and improve revenue performance.

---

## Key Business Insights

- Peak season (July-August) generates the highest ADR and booking demand.
- Online TA contributes the highest estimated revenue but also experiences relatively high cancellation rates.
- Longer lead times are associated with significantly higher cancellation risk.
- Different customer segments require different commercial strategies.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Apply dynamic pricing during peak seasons.
- Improve booking quality by promoting non-refundable offers for high-risk bookings.
- Continuously optimize channel performance using booking, ADR, and cancellation metrics.
- Provide data-driven recommendations to hotel partners to maximize long-term revenue.
